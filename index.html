"OLD BARN" is a home for everyone - young and old. For all those who want to step away from paved streets, who want to explore and preserve untouched nature, who want to make new friends at the camp fire. "OLD BARN" has a mission to provide education classes about local wildlife as well as traditional and sustainable hunting.
